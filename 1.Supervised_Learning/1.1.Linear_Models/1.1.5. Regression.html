  • R2_score

    • $R^{2} = 1 - \frac{SSR}{SST}$

      $SSR = \sum{(y_{i} - \hat{y_{i}})^{2}}$

      $SST = \sum{(y_{i} - \bar{y})^{2}}$
In [ ]:
pred = model.predict(X_test)

SSR = np.sum((y_test - pred)**2)
SST = np.sum((y_test - np.mean(y_test))**2)

R2 = 1 - SSR / SST
